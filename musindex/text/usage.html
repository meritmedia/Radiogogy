Usage

The use of the MusIndex application is at no charge. However, from time to time you will be gently - and somewhat randomly - persuaded to click on a banner ad. Please note that doing so will take you away from this site. However, clicking your browser's "Back" button will bring you back to MusIndex.

Half of the pennies earned from your click are used to pay the developer, the other half are donated to the "Be Your Own Hero Foundation", a non-profit organization whose goal is to raise awareness to one's own belief in themselves.

Thank you for your consideration and patronage.
